Welcome!

Leroy and Richards Automotive was established in 1975 with the vision of Leroy Winkler. He worked hard at being the best at his trade for 31 years. After his passing on December 15th 2006, his protege Richard Irwin Barton III took over the company and has continued to work hard to keep the company's good name.

Our technicians are trained and "ASE" certified. We back our work and our name is our guarantee. We would like to personally thank the customers who have continued their service here and we hope that each of you feel as though you are family to us. To those looking for a trustworthy place in the Corpus Christi area that will take care of all your car needs, stop by today and experience peace of mind knowing that your car will be fixed to your full satisfaction.

Our Services

Climate Control Systems

A climate control system is a complex system that requires routine maintenance for increased seasonal performance and operation. Services provided include: air conditioning system diagnostic, air conditioning system service, heating system diagnostic, heating system service, etc

Steering and Suspension

The steering and suspension system on your vehicle is responsible for maintaining comfortable riding conditions and driving performance. Services provided include: shocks, struts, ball joints, bushings, complete front suspension repair, full front and rear brake service, etc

Transmission Service

The transmission is the link between the engine and the wheels of your vehicle. Whether you drive a manual, automatic, or semi-automatic vehicle. Services provided include: clutch adjustment and inspection, clutch replacement, transmission replacement, etc

Electrical Systems

The electrical and electronic systems in your vehicle are responsible for routing power and information in order to dictate. Services provided include: dashboard warning diagnostic, headlight bulb replacement, interior and exterior lighting repair, power locks repair, etc

Preventive Maintenance

Preventive maintenance is the care and protection of your vehicle against potential major auto repairs. Services provided include: air filter replacement, fuel injection, oil and filter change, spring checkup, etc

Complete Tune Ups

Spark plugs, plug wires, PCV valve, distributor cap and rotor, fuel filter, air filter, etc
